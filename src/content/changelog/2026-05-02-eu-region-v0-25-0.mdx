EU region (eu.onetimesecret.com) was upgraded straight from v0.22.1 to v0.25.0, skipping the
gradual changes in v0.23 and v0.24. Prior to regional sites when there was just a onetimesecret.com, that became what is now referred to as the EU region (which made sense since it was already hosted in the EU). It was the last region to migrate, completing
a staggered rollout that started with the UK in March.

It was last but not least -- actually the opposite: it has the most data by far. It's been around since the dawn of time (over 10 years). Roughly 10x compared to the US which is the next largest.
