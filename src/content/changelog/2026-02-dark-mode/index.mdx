## How to switch

By default, the site follows your operating system's theme preference. If your OS is set to dark mode, Onetime Secret will automatically appear in dark mode.

You can also toggle between light and dark manually using the theme switcher in the footer of every page. Your preference is saved locally so it persists across visits.

<Callout type="info">
  Dark mode applies to the full site including the secret creation form, the secret viewing page, and your account dashboard.
</Callout>

The design uses carefully chosen contrast ratios to ensure readability in both modes. All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.
