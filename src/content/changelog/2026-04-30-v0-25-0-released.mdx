v0.25 builds on the v0.24 foundation with organizations and team member functionality.
This release establishes a new convention for implementing a feature per-install
(i.e. site-wide) and per-domain, where the YAML config documents a feature's settings
and provides the site-wide configuration; the per-domain settings are saved in the main
database.
