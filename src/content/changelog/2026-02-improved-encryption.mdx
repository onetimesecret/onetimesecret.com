We've upgraded the encryption used for all stored secrets to AES-256-GCM, a modern authenticated encryption standard. This applies to every secret created from now on, and all previously stored secrets have been transparently migrated.

## What this means for you

Nothing changes in how you use the service. You still create and share secrets the same way. The difference is under the hood: your data is now protected by a stronger, more modern encryption algorithm that provides both confidentiality and integrity verification.

<Callout type="info">
  This change was part of our regular security review cycle. No secrets were compromised — this is a proactive upgrade to stay ahead of evolving best practices.
</Callout>
