The UK region (uk.onetimesecret.com) went live as our fifth region, hosted on UpCloud
infrastructure and following the share-nothing architecture: data created in the UK
stays in the UK. The launch was timed to give UK a small data set and live traffic
ahead of the v0.24 rollout, which began here a month later.
