NZ region (nz.onetimesecret.com) was the second region to receive v0.24, picking up the
V1 API and authentication fixes that had landed since the UK deployment. The maintenance
window ran about 8 hours overnight, longer than we wanted. Subsequent rollouts (CA, US,
EU) tightened the cut-over considerably as the process matured.
