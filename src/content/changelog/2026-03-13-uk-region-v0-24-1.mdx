UK region (uk.onetimesecret.com) was the first region to receive v0.24, deployed on
v0.24.1 the day after it was tagged. UK was chosen to go first because it was the newest
region with the smallest data set and the lowest customer load. This deployment kicked
off the staggered cross-region rollout.
